Aadhaar Card Correction Guide

  1. Visit the official UIDAI correction portal: Update Aadhaar Details
  2. Create or log in to your Aadhaar account.
  3. Select the field(s) you want to update (e.g., name, address).
  4. Upload the required documents as proof.
  5. Submit the request and note your Update Request Number (URN).
  6. Track the correction status on the UIDAI site.

PAN Card Correction Guide

  1. Visit NSDL or UTIITSL PAN correction portal:
    NSDL PAN
  2. Fill the form with accurate details.
  3. Upload valid supporting documents (proof of identity, address, etc.).
  4. Pay the correction fee online.
  5. Submit and note the acknowledgement number for tracking.

Driving Licence Services Guide

  1. Go to the official Parivahan portal:
    Apply/Renew DL Online
  2. Select your state, then click “Apply Online” for New/Renewal/Correction.
  3. Fill out the online application and upload any required documents.
  4. Book appointment for biometrics/fingerprints (RTO visit may be needed).
  5. Track status using your application number.
